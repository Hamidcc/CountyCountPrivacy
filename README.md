# Privacy Policy for CountyCount
Effective Date: **January 1, 2026**

CountyCount (“we,” “our,” or “the Bot”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

CountyCount only collects minimal information necessary to operate the bot:

Discord Account ID – to identify and track users in the counting game.

Counting Data – including how many counts you have made and the last number you counted.

No other personal information, such as your name, email, or payment details, is collected.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely for the purpose of providing the bot’s functionality:

To track your counting progress and scores.

To display leaderboards and statistics within the bot.

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Retention

Your data is stored only as long as necessary to provide CountyCount’s services. If you stop using the bot, your counting data may be deleted after a reasonable period.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to request:

Access to the data we store about you.

Deletion of your data from our systems.

To exercise these rights, please contact the bot owner via Discord or the support server.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the “Effective Date” at the top of this page. Continued use of CountyCount after changes indicates your acceptance of the revised policy.

7. Contact

For questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or your data, you can contact the bot owner via Discord.
